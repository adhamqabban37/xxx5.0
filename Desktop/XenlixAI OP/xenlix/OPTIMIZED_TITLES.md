## CTR Optimization Principles Applied

### ‚úÖ Benefits-First Approach
- "Free AEO Audit" vs "XenlixAI | Get Found"
- "Business Intelligence" vs "Premium Business Intelligence"
- Leading with what user gets, not just what it is

### ‚úÖ Emotional Triggers
- "Free" - removes barrier
- "Expert Help" - builds trust
- "Real Results" - credibility
- "One-Click" - ease of use

### ‚úÖ Clear Value Proposition
- Each title clearly states what the page/tool does
- Benefits are specific, not generic
- Industry-relevant keywords included

### ‚úÖ Brand Consistency
- Standardized " | XenlixAI" suffix
- Consistent separator usage (|)
- Brand placement at end for better keyword prominence
